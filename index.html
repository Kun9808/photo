PHOTOGRAPHY

Hello. My name is Kunal Tambe,
I'm a Photographer in Pune & Mumbai

My Story:I’ve always had a love for capturing the beauty of the world around me, but it wasn’t until I picked up my first camera in 2015 that I realized I wanted to turn this passion into a career. Since then, I’ve been fortunate to work with amazing clients and travel to beautiful locations, each shoot bringing new challenges and rewarding experiences."

Photography Style:"My photography style is deeply influenced by natural light and a candid approach. I aim to capture real moments and emotions in every shot. I believe the best photos are the ones that tell a story and evoke feeling. Whether I’m photographing a couple on their wedding day or a family during a sunset shoot, I always prioritize creating a relaxed environment to bring out their true personalities."

"Photography is more than just taking pictures for me—it’s about freezing a moment in time and preserving memories that people can cherish forever. I love knowing that my photos bring joy and help clients relive their special moments over and over again."

"When I’m not behind the camera, you can find me hiking in the mountains, sipping coffee at a local café, or experimenting with my latest photography gear. I’m also a huge fan of Travelling, and I’m always up for an adventure."
